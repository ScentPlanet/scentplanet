# Hardware Guide

## ScentPlanet BLE Scent Device

ScentPlanet works with our companion Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) scent device.

### Specifications

| Spec | Detail |
|:-----|:-------|
| **Connectivity** | Bluetooth Low Energy 5.0 (BLE) |
| **Cartridges** | 6 interchangeable scent slots |
| **Power** | USB-C rechargeable |
| **Range** | Up to 10 meters wireless |
| **Compatibility** | Windows 10/11 with BLE support |

### Scent Cartridges

Each slot maps to a specific environment type. **Slot order is hardware-defined and cannot be changed.**

| Slot | Scent | Environment |
|:----:|:------|:------------|
| 0 | Fresh Grass | Grasslands, meadows, open fields |
| 1 | BBQ / Grill | Barbecue areas, dry heat environments |
| 2 | Pine / Wood | Forests, lumber areas, wooden structures |
| 3 | Campfire Smoke | Bonfires, fire pits, burning |
| 4 | Cooking Stew | Kitchens, cooking pots, food areas |
| 5 | Floral | Gardens, flower fields, botanical areas |

### Pairing

1. Power on your scent device (hold the button for 2 seconds)
2. Open ScentPlanet and navigate to **Settings > Device**
3. Click **Connect** — the app will scan for nearby BLE devices
4. Select your device from the list
5. Once paired, the status indicator turns green

### Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|:------|:---------|
| Device not found | Ensure Bluetooth is enabled and device is powered on |
| Connection drops | Move device closer (within 10m), avoid interference |
| Weak scent output | Check cartridge levels, ensure device is charged |
| No scent at all | Verify cartridges are properly seated in slots |

### Cartridge Replacement

1. Power off the device
2. Gently pull out the empty cartridge from its slot
3. Insert the new cartridge until it clicks
4. Power on — the device auto-calibrates
